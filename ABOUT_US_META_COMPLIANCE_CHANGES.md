## CRITICAL ISSUES (Must Fix Before Ad Launch)

### 1. EMOTIONAL OUTCOME CLAIMS ⚠️ HIGHEST RISK

**Problem:** Meta prohibits guaranteed emotional/psychological benefits without substantiation

**Current Problematic Text:**
```
"If you feel calm here, if something within you softens"
"brings peace to your heart"
"If you feel called, begin your journey with us"
```

**Why It's Flagged:** 
Meta's system interprets these as guaranteed emotional outcomes ("peace," "calm," "softening"). This is classified as an unsubstantiated psychological benefit claim—treating spiritual guidance like a therapeutic intervention.

**Suggested Rewrites:**

| Current | Revised | Reason |
|---------|---------|--------|
| "If you feel calm here, if something within you softens" | "If this resonates with your spiritual practice, we welcome you" | Removes outcome guarantee; focuses on alignment |
| "brings peace to your heart" | "may support your spiritual reflection" | Adds uncertainty qualifier |
| "If you feel called, begin your journey with us" | "If this path calls to you, explore our work with us" | Softens the outcome promise |

**Impact:** ✅ Removes guaranteed emotional outcome language while maintaining warmth

---

### 2. VULNERABLE POPULATION TARGETING ⚠️ HIGH RISK

**Problem:** Meta's Personal Attributes policy prohibits targeting people explicitly in vulnerable emotional states as a marketing angle

**Current Problematic Text (Section 5 - Why We Exist):**
```
There are many who feel lost in silence.
Many who seek reassurance without drama.
Many who want spiritual clarity, not fear.

This space exists for them.
For the quiet devotee.
For the inward seeker.
For the one who feels called, but does not know why.
```

**Why It's Flagged:** 
Meta classifies this as predatory targeting. You're explicitly saying "we target people who are lost and confused." Even though your intent is compassionate, Meta's system flags this as targeting vulnerable populations seeking quick answers.

**Suggested Complete Rewrite for Section 5:**

**CURRENT:**
```
There are many who feel lost in silence.
Many who seek reassurance without drama.
Many who want spiritual clarity, not fear.

This space exists for them.
For the quiet devotee.
For the inward seeker.
For the one who feels called, but does not know why.

If you feel calm here,
if something within you softens —
that is enough.
```

**REVISED:**
```
This space is for those drawn to contemplative wisdom.
For those interested in Vedic perspective on life patterns.
For those seeking spiritual understanding, not fear-based predictions.
For the curious mind.
For the sincere seeker.
For those aligned with our approach.

If this space resonates with your values,
if our perspective aligns with your practice —
we welcome your exploration.
```

**Why This Works:**
- Shifts from targeting emotional vulnerability ("lost," "calling but confused") 
- To targeting interest/philosophy ("Vedic wisdom," "contemplative," "sincere seeker")
- Describes the exact same people, but as a choice/interest, not a crisis
- Removes emotional outcome language

**Impact:** ✅ Transforms vulnerable targeting into interest-based targeting

### 3. UNSUBSTANTIATED CONSCIOUSNESS/DESTINY CLAIMS ⚠️ HIGH RISK

**Problem:** Meta's Misinformation policy requires factual claims to be supported or explicitly framed as belief/philosophy

**Current Problematic Text:**
- "Time, karma, and destiny are not accidents of the universe. They are movements of consciousness under the silent witness of Shiva."
- "Destiny transforms when awareness awakens"
- "When the soul asks sincerely, Shiva responds"

**Why It's Flagged:**
These are stated as metaphysical facts without framing as spiritual belief. Meta treats unframed metaphysical claims as potential misinformation. However, if you frame them as your spiritual tradition/philosophy, they're protected.

#### Suggested Rewrite for Section 2 - Our Foundation

**CURRENT:**
```
Everything here begins with one truth:
ॐ नमः शिवाय

We believe that time, karma, and destiny are not accidents of the universe. 
They are movements of consciousness under the silent witness of Shiva.

Our work is to observe, interpret, and present what is already written 
in the subtle language of time — with humility and surrender.
```

**REVISED:**
```
Everything here begins with one truth:
ॐ नमः शिवाय

From our Vedic tradition, we understand that time, karma, and destiny 
operate as movements of consciousness—observed through Shiva's awareness.

Our work is to interpret these patterns—not as fixed prophecy, but as 
spiritual perspective offering self-reflection and personal insight.
```

**Key Changes:**
- ❌ "We believe that" → ✅ "From our Vedic tradition, we understand"
- ❌ "movements of consciousness" → ✅ "movements of consciousness within our tradition"
- ❌ "already written" → ✅ "interpreted as spiritual perspective"
- Adds "self-reflection" framing = makes it about insight, not prediction

**Impact:** ✅ Frames claims as belief system, not metaphysical fact

### 4. BUSINESS MODEL OPACITY / BAIT-AND-SWITCH RISK ⚠️ HIGH RISK

**Problem:** Meta's Deceptive Practices policy prohibits misleading users about product/service nature or pricing

**Current Issues:**
- Section 1: "This platform was not created as a business idea. It arose as seva — sacred service."
- Section 5: "If you feel calm here... that is enough"
- CTA: "Explore Our Reports" (product suddenly appears)

**Why It's Flagged:**
Meta sees a contradiction: "not a business," "no product," "just a service"... but then "Explore Our Reports." This reads like you're hiding that you're selling something. That triggers deceptive practices flags.

**Solution A: Rewrite Section 1 for Clarity**

**CURRENT:**
```
This platform was not created as a business idea.
It arose as seva — sacred service.
```

**REVISED:**
```
This platform arose as seva — sacred service. 
To sustain this mission and serve more seekers, we offer 
Vedic reports and guidance as tools for spiritual reflection.
```

**Why This Works:**
- Explicitly states you have products/services upfront
- Reframes revenue as "sustaining the mission" (aligns with seva philosophy)
- Sets clear expectations before CTA

**Solution B: Add NEW Transparency Section**

**INSERT NEW SHORT SECTION** (place after Section 6 or before bottom CTA):

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A Note on Our Service

We offer Vedic astrology reports and spiritual guidance as the means to 
sustain our mission. When you explore our work with us, you're supporting 
a community dedicated to spiritual service. 

Our reports are tools for reflection within our Vedic tradition—not promises 
of life changes, but invitations to deeper self-understanding and personal insight.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

**Why This Works:**
- Explicitly states you have products/services = no "surprise" in CTA
- Reframes payment as "supporting the mission" = aligns with seva philosophy
- Clarifies reports are "tools for reflection" not guarantees = manages expectations
- Maintains authentic spiritual positioning
- Protects against "I didn't know I was paying for something" complaints

**Impact:** ✅ Removes deceptive practices flag
