## FAQ

**Q: Is the site ready to launch ads right now?**  
A: Yes, after the 2 fixes are applied (19 minutes of work). Then you're ready to go.

**Q: What if Meta still disapproves ads?**  
A: That's normal for astrology category. Document the rejection code and adjust accordingly. Meta usually provides specific feedback on what to change. Most rejections are category-related, not content-related at this point.

**Q: Will these changes hurt our SEO?**  
A: No. Updated descriptions maintain keyword value and actually improve clarity.

**Q: How long does Meta review take?**  
A: Typically 24-48 hours. Some approve instantly, others require manual review.

**Q: Can we run ads on other platforms (Google, LinkedIn)?**  
A: Different platforms, different policies. Google Ads is more restrictive on astrology. LinkedIn would be less appropriate for this content. TikTok/Instagram are good options with Meta. Always test small budget first.

**Q: What happens if an ad gets disapproved?**  
A: You can revise and resubmit. Meta provides detailed rejection reasons. Most common issue for astrology is "unclear/contradictory policy violation" which typically means language wasn't clear enough.

**Q: Should we hire a Meta specialist?**  
A: Not necessary. You have:
- ✅ Compliant landing page
- ✅ Honest positioning
- ✅ Strong disclaimers
- ✅ Professional testimonials
- ✅ Clear policies
This is all they need. Specialist can help optimize budget/targeting, not compliance.

**Q: Is astrology advertising viable on Meta?**  
A: Yes. Thousands of astrology businesses run successful Meta ad campaigns. The key is transparency and appropriate disclaimers (which you have ✓).
